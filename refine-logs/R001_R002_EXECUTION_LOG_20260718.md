## R002: exact translated target-shape geometry

### Purpose

Test whether a deterministic control can preserve exact target shape, area,
components, perimeter, compactness, aspect ratio, and vertical image band while
remaining inside content and pixel-disjoint from the expert target.

- Overall feasibility: `278/377 = 73.74%`.

### Analysis

The complete search substantially improves feasibility but still misses the
preregistered threshold. The residual failure is geometric rather than a score
or model result: broad expert masks, especially large high-consensus pleural
effusions, cannot be translated within the same vertical band without overlap.
Lowering the threshold from 90% to 88.89% after seeing the result would violate
the authority.
